AI Tank Personalities
---------------------

Cautious:

When the player is encountered, the AI will pursue the player if the AI health is greater than half,
otherwise it will flee from the player.


Cowardly:

When the player is encountered, the AI will flee flee the player.


Erratic:

When the player is encountered, the AI will randomly pursue the player or flee from the player.


Overconfident:

When the player is encountered, the AI will pursue the player.
